About Me

Welcome to my professional space! I am Himanshu Bhardwaj, the Founder & CEO of AICrux Business Solutions Pvt. Ltd., where I lead a dynamic team dedicated to revolutionizing the business landscape with cutting-edge analytics and machine learning solutions. With nearly a decade of extensive experience in managing national and international projects, my journey has been marked by a relentless pursuit of innovation and excellence in data processing and analytics.

My Academic Journey: I hold an MBA from the prestigious Indian Institute of Management, Shillong, which has honed my strategic business acumen and leadership skills. Complementing this, I earned an MTech in Applied Geophysics from the Indian Institute of Technology (ISM), Dhanbad, providing a robust technical foundation that underpins my expertise in advanced data analysis and geospatial technologies.

Professional Milestones: Throughout my career, I have spearheaded numerous high-impact projects. I created GeoBizML, a pioneering Python package for geospatial data visualization, machine learning, and analysis, enabling users to perform sophisticated geospatial analyses with ease. Additionally, I developed StoreFlow, a comprehensive ERP software designed for retail store management, featuring advanced analytics, automated financial reporting, and seamless point-of-sale integration.

Leadership and Innovation: At AICrux Business Solutions, I lead a talented team of software developers, designers, and marketing professionals, fostering a culture of continuous improvement and innovation. My leadership is driven by a commitment to optimizing operational efficiency and delivering high-quality software products that empower businesses to achieve their goals.

Beyond Work: I am also an avid online course creator on Udemy, where I share my knowledge and expertise in hands-on marketing analytics, machine learning, and AI applications in finance. My courses are designed to equip learners with practical skills and insights that can be directly applied to real-world challenges.

Certifications and Recognition: I am a certified Project Management Professional (PMP) by the Project Management Institute, which underscores my dedication to excellence in project management and strategic execution.

Vision: My vision is to drive organizational growth and success by leveraging the power of data and analytics. I am passionate about transforming raw data into actionable insights, fostering innovation, and delivering solutions that make a tangible impact.

Thank you for visiting my website. I invite you to explore my work, connect with me on LinkedIn, and join me on this exciting journey of data-driven discovery and innovation.
